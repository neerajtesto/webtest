An Indian Film Actor.

Hobbies Achievements

Actor Arya(born 11 december 1980) is an Indian actor and producer who predominantly appears in Tamil films.Arya has won the Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut-South and received two nominations each for Filmfare Awards and Vijay Awards.He was included on the 2015 edition of the Forbs India Celebrity 100, a list based on top earning Indian celebrities.He made his breakthrough portraying rogue characters in Vishnuvardhan's Arinthum Ariyamalum(2005) and Pattiyal (2006). He later won critical acclaim for playing an Aghori in Bala's Naan Kadavul (2009). Arya gained further commercial success with the release of the peiod drama Madrasapattinam(2010),the comedy film Boss Engira Bhaskaran(2010),family action film Vettai (2012) and Atlee's family film Raja Rani. Arya is the ambassador of television and print marketing campaigns of Otto,the flagship brand of Pothys Clothing. Arya started off as a computer engineer, his passion for filming brought him to modelling. He is also well in cycling, he participated inthe Vatternrundan Motala cycle race and won a medal.
